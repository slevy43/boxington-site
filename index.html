What we do

Boxington, as we know it today, started from a humble cabin on the French River in Ontario, Canada. During his brief business endeavour into the pub scene, Danny 'Smethwick' G. found a passion for the art of skateboarding while in the downtown London scene. After this experience, Danny wanted to spread the love he had for the art of skateboarding!

Since this revelation, Danny dedicated his craft to selling the highest quality shoes, clothes, and skategear designed by skaters, for skaters. His vision for his Boxington Skate brand company was to be able to welcome beginning skaters to the scene with stylish and affordable gear, but still appease seasoned skaters with its high-grade, well designed products. As we say, the only limit is the pavement.
